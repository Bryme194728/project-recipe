Description

It is usually made with beans, or if you choose to make it with rice, it will come out the same but different taste. But not only that, we also have some ingriedents

Ingriedents

  • rice
  • beans
  • pepper and tomato
  • salt and maggi

Procedures on how to make it

  1. Firstly, blend your beans or rice
  2. Secondly, put water on the gas and make it boil before you put your beans/rice
  3. Then you put your pepper and stir
  4. Also put your seasoning and leave for about 30 minutes
  5. Put in a plate and eat
